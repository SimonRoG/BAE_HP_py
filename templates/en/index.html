About us.

The BAE GROUP represents a multifaceted group of companies that is comprehensively dedicated to the life cycle of real estate. Our core competencies extend across eight strategic business areas that cover the entire spectrum of the real estate industry. From technical building services, contracting, and project management to digital solutions, asset management, property management, general planning and investment management - the BAE GROUP is a holistic partner for all aspects of your real estate.

With numerous dedicated employees, we form a dynamic team of real estate specialists covering different perspectives and disciplines. Our experts, be they engineers, technicians, architects, business economists or computer scientists, combine their skills to develop innovative and customized solutions for our clients.

The BAE GROUP is characterized not only by its wide range of services, but also by its regional presence and global perspective. Our locations in Germany and Eastern Europe enable us to respond flexibly to the needs of our customers and offer customized solutions for every challenge.
