About Us

The BAE GROUP represents a diverse corporate group dedicated to the entire lifecycle of real estate. Our core competencies span eight strategic business areas that cover the full spectrum of the real estate industry. From technical building equipment, contracting, and project management to digital solutions, asset management, property management, general planning, and investment management — the BAE GROUP is a comprehensive partner for all aspects of your real estate.

With a large team of dedicated employees, we form a dynamic group of real estate specialists who bring various perspectives and areas of expertise. Our experts — whether engineers, technicians, architects, business economists, or IT specialists — combine their skills to develop innovative and tailored solutions for our clients.

The BAE GROUP stands out not only for its broad range of services but also for its regional presence and global perspective. Our locations in Germany and Eastern Europe allow us to respond flexibly to the needs of our clients and provide customized solutions for every challenge.
