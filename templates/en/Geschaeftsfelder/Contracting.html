Contracting.

Energy contracting, also known as energy services or energy supply contracting, is a effective tool for increasing energy efficiency in buildings. With this approach an external service provider, the so-called contractor, takes over the planning, financing, Implementation and operation of energy efficiency measures. In return, the contractor receives one contractually agreed remuneration, which often results from the energy costs saved.

As an experienced partner in the field of energy contracting, we offer our business area Energy contracting comprehensive services, from the analysis of energy efficiency potential to long-term support of the installed systems.
