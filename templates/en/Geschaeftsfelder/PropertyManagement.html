Commercial property management focuses on financial and administrative Building management side. The aim is to ensure that financial processes run smoothly, rental income is maximized and Operating costs are managed efficiently. The distinction from asset management lies in the fact that Commercial property management focuses more on short-term financial concerns is and fewer strategic decisions over the entire life cycle of the assets meets.

In contrast, the focus of technical property management is physical Maintenance and optimization of buildings. This includes the maintenance of technical systems, Repairs, inspections and the implementation of modernization measures. The technical one Property management aims to extend the life of the building infrastructure To ensure operational reliability and maintain technical standards. Also The distinction from asset management can be seen here, as technical property management is focused on immediate physical management and less on strategic, financial decisions.

The advantage of commercial property management lies in efficient financial management and Budgeting, optimizing rental income and operating costs and creating Tenant satisfaction and retention through professional tenant management. The technical one Property management has the advantage of extending the lifespan of the property Building infrastructure, minimizing downtimes and operational disruptions and ensuring a high technical standard is averted.

Overall, both commercial and technical property management contribute to to maintain the value of buildings and to meet the requirements of tenants. With our property management business area, we offer numerous services to: To maintain and further optimize the value of buildings.
