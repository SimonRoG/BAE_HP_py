Project Management.

Project management in construction plays a crucial role in complex construction projects to plan, coordinate and complete successfully. It's like composing one Orchestra in which various professionals come together to work harmoniously on a common project The goal of working -in this case, the construction of a building.

The conductor in an orchestra is comparable to the project manager in construction. Both play a central role in coordinating and leading the team. The conductor gives it time and ensures that each musician plays at the right time and at the right tempo. Similarly, the project manager manages the construction project by ensuring that everyone involved are coordinated and carry out their tasks in a coordinated manner. The musicians in the orchestra represent the various construction experts, including architects, Engineers, craftsmen and other specialists. Every musician brings their own skills and Expertise, just as the construction experts use their unique skills to implement the contribute to the construction project.

The construction industry requires smooth cooperation between those involved in order to be successful to ensure the project. Project management plays the role of ensuring that the different professionals work together effectively and their individual skills be harmoniously integrated.
